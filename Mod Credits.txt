Credits for Tiberian Sun Faction Expansion Mod - ALPHA 2.3.7

[Goal]
The Main Goal of this Mod is to add addition factions to Tiberain Sun Client 5.46 and some minor Tweaks to gameplay.
The Factions added are Cabal and HSF.
The Cabal's faction is a split of Nod Tech with cybernetic units, Mutegenic Weapons, and Subterrianian Tech.
The HSF is a subfaction of GDI that specializes on experimental technology, HSF stands for Hammerfest Security Forces, They specialize in EMP, Hover, and Particle Weaponry.

[Mod State]
The Game is currently in Alpha but it has a fully functional Multiplayer and Skrimish menus, unless I missed something.
The Campaign currently does not work and is disable by default.
The Mod Standalone which mean that the mod doesn't require the base Client.

[Assets]
The Mod also replaces unit sprites and voxels with community assets and my custom assets.
The Mod also contains Randoms Maps that I converted from Tiberian Sun.
